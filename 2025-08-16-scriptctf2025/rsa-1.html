From the description, we guess that the same message is encrypted for three times, so:

  • msg ^ 3 mod n1 = c1
  • msg ^ 3 mod n2 = c3
  • msg ^ 3 mod n3 = c3

We can solve msg ^ 3 via chinese remainder theorm:
